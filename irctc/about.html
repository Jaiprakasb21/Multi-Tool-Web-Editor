Understanding IRCTC Booking Rules

Indian Railways allows advance booking of train tickets up to 60 days (excluding the date of journey). The booking window opens daily at 8:00 AM IST.

Example: On January 1st at 8:00 AM, tickets for March 2nd become available for booking.

Our tool helps you understand these rules and plan your bookings accordingly, ensuring you never miss the booking window for your journey.
