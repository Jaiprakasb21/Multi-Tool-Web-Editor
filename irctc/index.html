Understanding IRCTC Booking Window & 60 Days Rule

What is IRCTC Advance Reservation Period?

The IRCTC advance reservation period is the timeframe during which passengers can book train tickets before their journey date. Currently, Indian Railways allows booking up to 60 days in advance, excluding the date of journey. This is commonly known as the IRCTC 60 days rule.

IRCTC Booking Window Opening Time - 8 AM IST

The IRCTC booking window opens daily at 8:00 AM IST (Indian Standard Time). This means that every morning at 8 AM, new journey dates become available for booking. For example:

  • On January 1st at 8:00 AM, tickets for March 2nd open
  • On February 15th at 8:00 AM, tickets for April 16th open
  • The pattern continues for all dates throughout the year

❓ Frequently Asked Questions About IRCTC Booking

What is the IRCTC advance booking period?

IRCTC allows passengers to book train tickets up to 60 days in advance (excluding the date of journey). This is the maximum advance reservation period set by Indian Railways.

When does the IRCTC booking window open?

The IRCTC booking window opens daily at 8:00 AM IST. For example, on January 1st at 8 AM, tickets for March 2nd become available. This timing is consistent throughout the year.

Can I book IRCTC tickets for dates beyond 60 days?

No, IRCTC does not allow booking beyond 60 days in advance. You need to wait until the date falls within the booking window. Use our checker to know exactly when booking opens for your journey date.

What time zone is used for IRCTC booking?

All IRCTC bookings follow Indian Standard Time (IST). The booking window opens at 8:00 AM IST regardless of your location. Our tool automatically handles IST timezone calculations.
