❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What is the IRCTC advance booking period?

IRCTC allows passengers to book train tickets up to 60 days in advance (excluding the date of journey).

When does the booking window open?

The booking window for new dates opens daily at 8:00 AM IST. For example, on January 1st at 8 AM, tickets for March 2nd become available.

Can I book tickets for dates beyond 60 days?

No, IRCTC does not allow booking beyond 60 days in advance. You need to wait until the date falls within the booking window.

What time zone is used for booking?

All IRCTC bookings follow Indian Standard Time (IST). The booking window opens at 8:00 AM IST regardless of your location.
