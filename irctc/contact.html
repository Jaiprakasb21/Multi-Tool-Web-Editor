Frequently Asked Questions

How do I use the IRCTC Booking Window Checker?

Simply select your journey date using the date picker on our homepage and click "Check Booking Status". The tool will instantly tell you if booking is open, when it will open, and provide a countdown timer.

Is this service free?

Yes! Our IRCTC Booking Window Checker is completely free to use. No registration, no hidden fees, no subscriptions required.

Do you store my journey date information?

No, we do not store any journey dates you check. All calculations are done in your browser, and no data is sent to our servers.

Can I book tickets through your website?

No, we only provide information about booking availability. When booking is open, we provide a direct link to the official IRCTC website where you can book your tickets.

Is this tool affiliated with IRCTC?

No, we are an independent tool and not affiliated with Indian Railways or IRCTC. We provide information based on publicly available IRCTC booking rules.

What if the booking rules change?

We monitor IRCTC policies regularly and update our tool accordingly. If you notice any discrepancies, please contact us immediately.

Can I suggest new features?

Absolutely! We welcome feedback and feature suggestions. Email us at support@propsanchal.com with your ideas.

How accurate is the countdown timer?

Our countdown timer is synchronized with Indian Standard Time (IST) and is accurate to the second. It automatically accounts for timezone differences.
